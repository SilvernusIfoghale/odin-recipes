Almond Recipe

This is a very tasty almond recipe, its one of its kind, very delicious and highly rich in fibre, so nutritious and refreshing,.....

Ingredient

  • Milk
  • Sugar
  • Raisin

Steps

  1. Get all the required ingredients
  2. Pill open the almond and rasin respectively
  3. Remove the seed inside rasin and dispose of it
  4. Blend both the raisin and almond to smooth
  5. Add milk and stir together to mix
  6. All ready, yummy.
